Who We Are

Phitech Solutions Limited (PSL), based in the UK, partners with Phi Tech Solutions (PTS) in Pakistan, founded in 2005. PTS provides competitive simulation, optimization, and analytics solutions for the process industry through a partnership with Aspen Technology Inc. Over two decades, PTS has become a leader in technology solution sales and Industry 4.0 implementation, delivering system integration projects and developing software in automation, system integration, and industrial cybersecurity. PSL globally represents PTS's service capabilities and smart software products.

Our Services & Working Process from Our Agency

Industrial Cybersecurity

A sound Industrial Cybersecurity strategy is essential for connected manufacturing plants. While IT Cybersecurity has been a focus for years, securing the OT network is a newer area. Our solutions address multiple aspects of Industrial Cybersecurity for OT networks, including health monitoring, air-gapping, and threat detection, through partnerships with hardware and software providers.

Process Design And Simulation

We have developed deep expertise in process simulation and design software like Aspen HYSYS, Aspen Plus, Aspen Exchanger Design & Rating, and Aspen Flare System Analyzer. These tools, essential for designing new plants and equipment, are used primarily to debottleneck and optimize existing assets, creating measurable business value.

Refinery Planning and Scheduling

Operating a profitable crude oil refinery is complex due to managing processing units, diverse crude oils, and fluctuating prices. Aspen's Planning and Scheduling software optimizes refinery operations using advanced algorithms for maximum profitability. Since 2005, we specialize in refinery planning, customizing systems, training personnel, and ensuring economic success globally. Our expansion via AspenTech's Independent Services Provider program aims to offer these specialized services worldwide.

Real Time Data Analytics

We specialize in helping process manufacturing companies leverage their plant data effectively. Our solutions focus on collecting, storing, and analyzing crucial data to drive decision-making and optimize manufacturing assets. Utilizing AspenTech's Data Works family of products, we enable real-time deployment of predictive models and dashboards, translating data into actionable insights for enhanced operational efficiency.

Reliability Simulation

With the increasing cost of capital, it is even more critical than before for asset-intensive industries (i.e., process industry) to maximize asset utilization of their existing assets, before embarking on a capital investment program. AspenTech Asset Performance Management suite of solutions uses advanced techniques such as reliability simulation and machine-learning to maximize utilization for not only at the equipment level, but also at the level of the overall site.

Hybrid Modeling

Combining data-driven machine learning with first-principles modeling presents new opportunities in the process industry. AspenTech’s Industrial AI solutions utilize this approach for unit operation models, mechanistic phenomena models, and real-time deployment. We leverage modern AI with first-principles tools to deliver effective modeling results.

Plant Pipping and Layout

Using Aspen OptiPlant 3D Layout software, we offer solutions to digitalize the layout of process plants. While traditional methods of 2-D drawings and plans will always be in use, this next-generation 3D technique provides a great alternative that connects better with the way the human mind visualizes a real plant. Aspen OptiRouter software extends the capability of Aspen OptiPlant 3D by automatically suggesting optimum piping routes to reduce costs.

Offshore Services Provisions

With access to the cost-effective, high quality talent pool of engineers in Pakistan, we provide services to our customers in the middle east and other regions. These consist of relatively repetitive “low-end” work (e.g., creating drawings) to specialized and high-end services (e.g., refinery planning model development).

Partnerships

Aspen Tech Channel Partner

As a channel partner of AspenTech since 2005, we have demonstrated product, industry, and system integration expertise to deliver complete solutions to our customers. This includes understanding customer requirements, discovery of opportunities and high value problems, technology selection, commercial contracts, support, and implementation services, amongst others.

Aspen Tech Independent Service Provider

While we have been providing implementation services for AspenTech products (especially real-time systems) for over a decade, we have formally joined the AspenTech Independent Services Provider (ISP) Network recently. As an ISP, we are now expanding into other markets globally with a view to help fill the shortage of Industry 4.0 skillset that is prevalent today and expected to remain a bottleneck to industry adopting and realizing its vision of the future.

Aspen Tech Training Partner

Key to growing a software product business is to enable its user-base to use the product effectively through official, certified training courses and programs. We have been a Training partner of AspenTech and have delivered over 100 corporate and public training courses since 2005. The training programs cover all aspects of learning, including concept-building, demonstrations, hands-on workshops, group discussion and certification, amongst other things.
